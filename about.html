Hello! My name is Tiffany Chen,




I am a ChineseAmerican girl working towards a degree in Digital Media Arts. I've always been into art and plan to continue pursuing art in the future. I became more interested in digital design and video editing throughout my time in college. I even learned a lot about photoshopping, 3D modeling, and coding! Although my specific future goal is still unclear, I know my heart longs for creativity.
